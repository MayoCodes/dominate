How Ghost works
Quiet.
Precise. Fast.

Ghost runs entirely inside your browser. Nothing leaves your device. It watches the page, not you.

01 — Install
Add to your browser in 30 seconds
Ghost installs as a standard browser extension. No account required to start scanning. No permissions beyond reading the active tab.
02 — Detect
Scan any page with one click
Click the Ghost icon on any social media page or article. Ghost analyses the text for AI writing patterns and returns a confidence score in under two seconds.
03 — Understand
See what triggered the detection
Ghost highlights the specific phrases and patterns that flagged as AI-generated. You learn the tells — and over time you start spotting them yourself.
